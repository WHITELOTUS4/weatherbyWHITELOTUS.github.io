Drive link

If you wish to provide a Google Drive link as the download source for your document, here's a quick guide on how to upload a file to Google Drive and share it:
1. Upload a File to Google Drive:
- Visit Google Drive and sign in with your Google account.
- Click the "+ New" button on the left sidebar and select "File upload."
- Browse your computer for the document you want to upload and select it.
- The file will be uploaded to your Google Drive.
2. Share the File with Us:
- Once the file is uploaded, right-click the file in your Google Drive.
- Select "Share" from the context menu that appears.
- In the "Share with people and groups" box, enter the email address or Google account of our platform (if provided) in the "People" field.
- Choose the sharing permissions from the dropdown (e.g., "Viewer" if you want to share it for viewing only).
- Click "Send" to share the file with us.
Remember to ensure that the sharing settings are appropriately configured to allow access to the intended audience. By following these steps, you can seamlessly provide your document through Google Drive, facilitating easy and secure access for our community.
